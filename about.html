It's Nice to Meet You!

At first we began with an idea to create a website that would allow victims of disaster find help close to them and allow others to volunteer in disaster relief. Along the way, we figured that it would be best to do the project incrementally and that's exactly what this project became. While organizations already do a good job of putting up postings for volunteering positions, we thought it would be great if there was a single, centralized place for all of that information. Primary features include the ability for organizations to make postings and for potential volunteers to search for any openings in their area.
